OUR GOALS

The Women Defenders is a safe space where the effects of gender inequality on mental health are highlighted. Our first goal is to empower non-men of all ages and races.

Our second goal is to make visible a topic such as mental health that is often taboo in order to create an environment where our public can feel safe.

Our third goal is to provide up-to-date statistics on this complex subject in a didactic and easy-to-direct manner for our young target audience.
